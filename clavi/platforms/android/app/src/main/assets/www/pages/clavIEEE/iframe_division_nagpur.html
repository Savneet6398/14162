IEEE Nagpur Division is one of the Divisions (Hubs) of IEEE Bombay Section.

The Interim Chair of IEEE Nagpur Proposed Subsection is Dr. Preeti Bajaj ( Secretary - IEEE India Council 2017, Vice Chair SAC - IEEE India Council (2013 -2016), Subcommittee Lead -Internships & Skill Development - IEEE R10 Adhoc Committee 2016).

The first Division Student Representative (DSR) of the IEEE Nagpur Division is Er. Kirtiraj R Garud (Team Lead-SB Networking Coordinator - IEEE India Council SAC 2017, Lead Developer IPR4Students.org).
